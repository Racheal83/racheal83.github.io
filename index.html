Vintage Couture Sales Analysis Dashboard

Overview

This project analyzes sales performance and customer demographics for Vintage Couture, a boutique retailer specializing in sustainable and retro fashion. The business operates both online and across multiple cities, offering unique vintage apparel. The goal of the analysis is to deliver clear, actionable insights through an interactive Excel dashboard to support strategic decisions in inventory planning, customer engagement, and marketing.


Objectives

  • 📊 Calculate total revenue and identify top-performing months
  • 🧍‍♂️ Classify customers by age group and gender
  • 🛍️ Compare performance across product categories and regions
  • 💳 Analyze differences in sales channels
  • 📈 Present insights in a dynamic, filterable dashboard


🧼 Data Cleaning & Preparation

  • 🔗 Merged Gender and Payment Method using Customer ID with VLOOKUP.
  • 🗺️ Merged State and Region using Location ID with VLOOKUP.
  • 🧠 Applied IF and IFS statements to categorize customers by Age Group:
    • 18–30 → Young Adult
    • 31–40 → Adult
    • 41–49 → Middle-aged Adult
    • 50+ → Senior
  • 📈 Enabled grouping by Age Group for revenue trend analysis.

📦 Office Supply Sales Analysis

📖Overview

This project is a business intelligence analysis of an office supply company’s sales, revenue, and profit data — conducted to uncover patterns, identify underperforming segments and regions, and recommend actionable steps to improve profitability while reducing tax burdens.

🎯 Objective & Problem Statement

The company needed clarity on which customer segments, products, and regions drove profitability and which contributed losses. Despite high sales, some segments showed negative profits after tax. The project aimed to answer these questions and guide strategic decisions.

📊 Data Description

  • Order Date: Date of each transaction
  • Segment: Small Business, Government, Enterprise, etc.
  • State: Lagos, Oyo, Ogun, etc.
  • Product: Pen, Notepad, Markers, etc.
  • Metrics: Sales, Profit, Tax, Revenue, Discounts

🛠 Tools & Methodology

  • Excel for data cleaning & preparation.
  • Power BI for dashboarding & interactive analysis.
  • Metrics calculated: Profitability, Revenue, Tax impact, Product & Regional performance.
  • Time-based analysis: monthly & quarterly trends, year-over-year.
  • DAX queries for computed measures, including:
    • Total Revenue = SUM(Sales[Revenue])
    • Total Profit = SUM(Sales[Profit])
    • Gross Sales = SUM(Sales[Gross Sales])
    • Tax on Profit = SUM(Sales[Tax])
    • Profit Before Tax = SUM(Sales[Profit]) + SUM(Sales[Tax])
    • Segment Contribution % = DIVIDE( SUM(Sales[Profit]), CALCULATE(SUM(Sales[Profit]), ALL(Sales[Segment])), 0)
    • Product Contribution = DIVIDE( SUM(Sales[Gross Sales]), CALCULATE(SUM(Sales[Gross Sales]), ALL(Sales[Product])), 0)

🔍 Key Insights

  • Small Business segment contributed the most profit at ₦13.83M.
  • Midmarket and Channel Partners had negative profitability after tax.
  • Top products: Pen, Notepad, Markers; Top regions: Lagos, Oyo.
  • Identified profitability traps in certain high-sales, low-profit products & segments.
